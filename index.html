About SwiftInk

A Nairobi print studio built on quality, speed, and human service.

SwiftInk helps families, couples, businesses, churches, schools, and event organisers turn important information into beautiful printed pieces.

Our story

Print support that feels personal, especially when the job matters.

Based in Nairobi, SwiftInk was created to make professional printing easier for clients who need both good design and reliable turnaround. Some customers arrive with ready artwork. Others only have photos, wording, or a rough brief. In both cases, we guide the job from idea to finished print.

What makes SwiftInk different is the combination of quality output, honest timelines, and attentive service. For sensitive projects like eulogies, we know families need calm communication, careful proofreading, and fast delivery.

For business and event work, we focus on sharp colour, practical material guidance, and finishes that match the final use.
